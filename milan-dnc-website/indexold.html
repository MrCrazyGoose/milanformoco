The plan
Fighting for a party that…
Organizes everywhere and year-round
In these trying times, we cannot leave any community behind, any time unused, or any potential untapped.
  • Invest in year-round field work in District 15 and countywide, working with precinct leaders and clubs.
  • Prioritize precinct-level and club-based organizing and building strong volunteer pipelines.
  • Establish campaign training programs, organize midterm phonebanks and canvasses.
Stands up to Trump & MAGA
Our communities are under attack, and we have to fight back.
  • Build digital rapid response capacity and rebuild the central committee's social media presence.
  • Unite organizations, communities, and campaigns against Trump's action.
  • Make the local party a force for action, from protests to phonebanks.
Build a party that works for the people
Make our party more representative and focused on delivering real results.
  • Move towards special elections from the appointment system.
  • Host regular town halls, record meetings, and open a feedback form for the party.
  • Show up to local events and maintain regular communication with community leaders.
Defends our communities & rejects hate
A party that reflects our diversity and protects our most vulnerable.
  • Stand up for immigrant communities with consistent advocacy and presence.
  • Reject antisemitism and hate clearly and consistently.
  • Work closely with our clubs to ensure all voices are heard, including high school, college, and young Democrats.
